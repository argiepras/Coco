Cold Conflict is an MMO nation simulation game placed at the height of the cold war.


Oppress your populace as a dictator, or rule with a velvet glove in a free democracy.


Free the proletariat from the bourgeoisie as a communist, or fight back against the red menace as a capitalist.


Develop peacefully through resource production and technological breakthroughs, or plunder the riches of other nations for yourself.


Ally or conspire against hundreds of other nations, each led by a player like you, in a sandbox political world driven by the players.
